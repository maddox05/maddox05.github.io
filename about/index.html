My Story


I've been coding for about 5 years now and it has always enticed me. I want to work on something that will benefit me outside of work. That's why I'm pursuing computer science at Purdue - while working you can learn things to go back and make personal projects.


I've always found it fun working on personal projects, like my first project, making a rust script that combatted recoil. It was so fun seeing my script work, and seeing my friends use it made it even better.


I don't want to work for someone else on something I don't care about. I want to do something great.

About This Site


This website serves as my personal portfolio and blog. Here you'll find my projects, thoughts on software development, and a glimpse into my life outside of coding. I built this site from scratch to showcase my work and share my journey as a developer.


The site features a neo-brutalist design aesthetic with bold typography, high-contrast colors, and interactive elements. Every piece of it was hand-coded by me.

Interests
